1. Getting Started

Sawad Finnix lets you apply for a personal loan and manage repayments from your phone. To use the app you will need:

  • A valid Thai national ID card.
  • A mobile phone number that can receive an SMS one-time code (OTP).
  • Recent bank statements (PDF, PNG, or JPG) for your loan application.
  • An internet connection.

The very first time you open the app you will go through a one-time onboarding and identity check. After that, you simply sign in with your PIN (or fingerprint / Face ID).

2. First-Time Setup & Identity Verification

When you launch the app for the first time, follow these steps:

Step 1 — Phone number. Enter your mobile phone number and tap continue. We send a 6-digit OTP code by SMS.
Step 2 — Enter the OTP. Type the 6-digit code from the SMS. If you didn't receive it, wait for the timer and request a new code.
Step 3 — Thai ID information. Confirm the identity details shown so we can verify you with ThaiID.
Step 4 — ThaiID verification. The app opens the official ThaiID verification flow. Complete it there; you'll be sent back to the app automatically when it succeeds.
Step 5 — Success. Once verified, your profile is created and you continue to set up a PIN.
Privacy: Your identity is verified through ThaiID. Your PIN is never stored on our servers — it stays securely on your device only.

3. Creating Your PIN

After verification you create a 6-digit PIN. This PIN protects access to the app each time you open it.

  • Enter your chosen 6 digits, then re-enter them to confirm.
  • Choose something memorable but not easy to guess (avoid 123456 or your birth year).
  • The PIN is encrypted and stored only on your device.

When finished, you arrive at the Home screen.

4. Signing In (Returning Users)

The next time you open the app you go straight to sign-in:

Fingerprint / Face ID. If you enabled biometrics, the app prompts for it automatically. Authenticate to enter.
PIN. You can always fall back to entering your 6-digit PIN.

If biometrics are available on your phone but not yet turned on, the app invites you to enable them for faster sign-in. On a successful sign-in, your latest profile and loan information are refreshed from the server.

5. Applying for a Loan

From the Home screen, start a loan request. The application is a guided, multi-step form:

StepWhat you do
PermissionsAllow the device permissions the app requests (e.g. contacts, location). These support your application.
ID cardReview the ID details pulled from your verified ThaiID profile.
Personal infoConfirm and complete your personal details.
ContactsProvide reference contact information.
StatementsUpload your bank statements (PDF / PNG / JPG, up to 10 MB each). You can see upload progress and remove a file if needed.
BankEnter the bank account for loan disbursement.
Summary & consentReview everything, agree to the consents, and submit.
Tip: Have your bank statement files ready on your phone before you start so the upload step is quick.

After you submit, your request is reviewed and, once approved, becomes an active loan that appears on your Home screen.

6. Viewing Your Loan

Your active loan is shown as a card on the Home screen with:

  • Outstanding balance.
  • Progress (installments paid out of the total term).
  • Your monthly installment amount.
  • The next due date.

Tap the card to open Loan Detail, which shows the applicant name, your masked ID, the full month-by-month payment schedule, the split between principal and interest, and how much you have paid so far.

7. Making a Payment

Step 1 — Open payment. From the Home card or Loan Detail, choose to make a payment.
Step 2 — Choose amount. Pay your regular installment, or enter a custom amount (it cannot exceed your outstanding balance).
Step 3 — Pick a channel. Select a payment channel.
Step 4 — Pay by QR. The app shows a QR code for the exact amount. You can save the QR image to your photo gallery to scan it in your banking app.
Step 5 — Confirmation & receipt. Once the payment is confirmed, the app records it, updates your installments and balance, and shows a receipt with the amount paid and remaining balance.

When your final payment brings the balance to zero, the loan is marked as closed.

8. Troubleshooting & FAQ

I didn't receive the OTP code.

Check your signal, wait for the resend timer, then request a new code. Make sure the phone number you entered is correct.

ThaiID verification didn't bring me back to the app.

Make sure you completed the verification in the ThaiID flow. If the app didn't reopen automatically, switch back to Sawad Finnix manually and retry.

I forgot my PIN.

Use biometric sign-in if you enabled it. If you cannot sign in, reinstall and complete onboarding again with the same Thai ID and phone number.

My statement file won't upload.

Confirm the file is a PDF, PNG, or JPG and under 10 MB. Check your internet connection and try again.

The app asks for permissions — do I have to allow them?

Permissions such as contacts and location help process your application. Some are optional depending on your device and platform. If you previously denied a permission, the app can take you to your phone's Settings to enable it.

Is my data safe?

Your PIN never leaves your device. Identity verification is handled through ThaiID, and your documents are stored securely.
